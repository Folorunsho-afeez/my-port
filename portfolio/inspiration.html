Life Stories of Legendary Law Scholars

Prof. Bola Ajibola (1934–2023)

A giant in Nigerian law, Prof. Bola Ajibola was a former Attorney General and Minister of Justice. Known for his integrity and dedication to justice, he served as a judge at the International Court of Justice. His journey from humble beginnings to global legal influence reminds us that perseverance and principles always pay off.

Chief Gani Fawehinmi (1938–2009)

An outspoken human rights lawyer and activist, Chief Gani Fawehinmi fought tirelessly against corruption and injustice in Nigeria. His boldness and unwavering commitment inspire every student to stand for truth, no matter the odds.

Justice Aloma Mariam Mukhtar

Nigeria’s first female Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, Justice Mukhtar broke barriers and paved the way for women in law. Her legacy teaches us to aim high and shatter ceilings.

Study Tips for Law Students

  1. Stay organized: Keep notes and materials tidy for quick review.
  2. Practice past questions regularly.
  3. Form study groups for different perspectives.
  4. Balance study with breaks to avoid burnout.
  5. Connect theory with real-world legal issues.
